What is Speaky Nook?

More than just a language class, we are a community.

Our Purpose

Speaky Nook is an English conversation club designed for those who want to practice speaking in a relaxed, friendly environment. We focus on communication, confidence, and community rather than grammar perfection.

  • Practice English in a relaxed environment
  • Meet people with similar interests
  • Discuss books, culture, movies, and everyday topics
  • Learn through conversation and shared experiences
